About

Originally established as a multi-unit business in 1994 with an initial capacity of just 7,000 spindles, we have grown immensely. Over the years, we have vertically integrated to cater to retail markets globally.

Infrastructure

Spinning Division

Constant modernization and technology upgrades make us a leading yarn manufacturer. Placements of machineries and spindles are well planned in strategic locations with economies of scale in mind.

  • Spindles: 2,40,000
  • Product Range: Ne 30s to 120s Combed and Compact Single Yarn

Weaving Division

Producing greige fabric tailored for the finest bedlinens available with the latest Picanol Omni Summum looms.

  • Production per year: 3 million meters
  • Thread Count: 100 TC to 1500 TC
  • Fabric Weaves: Plain, Twill, Sateen, Stripes, Oxford, Dobby

Bedlinen Division

Catering to retail, institutional, and utility bedding sectors globally with designs in solid and printed products.

  • Production Capacity: 1000 sheet sets a day
  • Fiber Composition: Egyptian cotton, Supima, Organic, Tencel, Linen

Corporate Social Responsibility

"It is not enough to be compassionate – you must act."

Safety and Well-Being

We are committed to caring for our employees and their safety is our priority. We take initiative to support them through constant training, workshops and educational programs. We provide family housing for out-of-state workers, bus transportation, hostel facilities for women employees, and canteen facilities for all.

Medical Projects

Our latest Kidney Dialysis project provides 70% low-cost kidney Dialysis services to unprivileged patients suffering from chronic kidney disease. The facility substantially reduces the financial burden for the patients who need the treatment several times a week.

Education

We constantly thrive for social transformation and engage with the community by adopting neighboring schools and providing them with necessary infrastructure like smart classrooms, regular classrooms, and toilet facilities. Overall we actively participate in their development and well-being.
