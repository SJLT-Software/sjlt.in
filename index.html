About Us

, originally established as a multi-unit business in 1994 with an initial capacity of just 7,000 spindles, has grown immensely. We are vertically integrated to cater to retail markets globally.

With our core strength in spinning fine yarns, we proudly deliver finished products that stand apart from our competitors and bring unmatched value to our customers.

Our Diversified Group

  • Logistics: Contractors of bulk transportation.
  • Granites: Meticulous quality control and shade-selection.
  • Water Division: Wholesale mineral water distribution.
  • Construction: Contractors for infrastructure projects.

Sustainability Efforts

Go Green and Save Nature. 50% of our energy requirements are met from our own renewable sources, with a goal of achieving 100%.

  • Wind Energy: 8.3 MW capacity wind farms.

Eco-Initiatives

  • Better Cotton Initiative: Proud members promoting sustainable farming practices.
  • Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD): Ensures no harmful effluents enter the environment.
